Welcome to Trading Card Event!

Hearthstone is a classic card game with a story background based on the world view of the Warcraft series. Players can choose ten professional "heroes" as the protagonist, similar to the professions in World of Warcraft. Collect cards and assemble your deck (30 cards) to defeat your opponents in 1v1! After banning 2 professions, each player starts to choose 3 professions to play the game. Win the game and have fun!
